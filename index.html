Our Proven Framework

The Host Blueprint — 5 Phases

A step-by-step system built from real experience running a successful UK short-term rental portfolio.

Phase 01

Foundations & Mindset

Before picking a property, you need the right foundations. We cover business setup, legal compliance, planning permissions, and the mindset shifts that separate successful operators.

  • Business registration and structure
  • Understanding the STR legal landscape
  • Planning permission and council rules
  • Building your success blueprint

Phase 02

Finding Opportunities

Learn how to identify high-demand areas, approach landlords with confidence, and analyse whether a deal is actually profitable — before you spend a penny.

  • Market research and location analysis
  • Approaching landlords — scripts and strategies
  • Profitability calculators and deal assessment
  • Co-hosting vs rent-to-rent — which fits you

Phase 03

Setup & Operations

Set up your property for maximum bookings and automate your operations so the business runs without you having to be available 24/7.

  • Furnishing on a budget for maximum impact
  • Professional listing creation and photography tips
  • Guest automation and messaging templates
  • Cleaning rotas and maintenance systems

Phase 04

Marketing & Bookings

Get more bookings, at higher prices, with less effort. Master Airbnb's algorithm, pricing tools, and multi-platform listing strategy.

  • Airbnb SEO and ranking optimisation
  • Dynamic pricing strategies
  • Booking.com and direct booking channels
  • Reviews, ratings and reputation management

Phase 05

Growth & Scaling

Once your first property is profitable, learn how to replicate the model — expanding your portfolio, hiring a team, and building lasting wealth through property.

  • Systems for managing multiple properties
  • Building a virtual team
  • Reinvesting profits to scale faster
  • Long-term wealth building strategies
