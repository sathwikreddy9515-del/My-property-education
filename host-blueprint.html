Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to own property to join this programme?+
No — that's the whole point. Co-hosting means managing someone else's property for a percentage of revenue. Rent-to-rent means leasing a property and listing it on Airbnb for full profit. Neither requires you to own property.
How quickly can I start earning?+
Most students secure their first property or co-hosting client within 90 days. Some move faster, some slower — it depends on your commitment. Your personal coach will help you move as quickly as possible.
I work full time — do I have time for this?+
Yes. The video modules are self-paced so you watch them in your own time. Weekly coaching calls are recorded if you can't attend live. Most students spend 5-10 hours per week on the programme.
What is a personal coach and what do they do?+
Every student is assigned a personal coach when they join. Your coach checks in regularly, reviews your progress, answers questions between live calls, and helps you stay accountable throughout the programme.
What is the difference between co-hosting and rent-to-rent?+
Co-hosting: You manage someone else's property on Airbnb and earn a percentage of revenue (typically 15-25%). Lower risk, no upfront rent. Rent-to-rent: You lease a property from a landlord at a fixed monthly rent, list it on Airbnb and keep all profit above your costs. Higher earning potential, more responsibility.
What happens on the free strategy call?+
The call is 45 minutes with a member of our team. We learn about your situation and goals, walk you through the programme, answer every question, and both sides decide if it's the right fit. No hard sell — we only work with people we know we can help.
Is there a community I can connect with?+
Yes — all students get access to our private Discord community with 200+ active members. Share progress, ask questions, get feedback on deals and connect with people at every stage of their journey.
How is the programme delivered?+
Everything is online. 22 video modules on any device, weekly live Zoom coaching calls, private Discord community, and all templates available to download immediately on joining.
